## What is Defang?

Defang is a radically **simpler** way for developers to create, deploy, and manage cloud applications. Defang offers the **easiest** way for you to create and deploy your containerized applications to the **WORLD**!

## What is Nextra?

A **simple** and **flexible** site generation framework with everything you love from Next.js.
